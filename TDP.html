The Data Professional

Learning Outcomes

  • Examine relevant industry practices of data science operations in order to build key skillsets for analysing and transforming large data sets in response to different business problems and to different needs.
  • Develop knowledge of fundamental programming concepts and problem-solving techniques used in data analysis, using suitable programming languages such as Python and SQL.
  • Develop an ethos of continuous professional development in the area of data science that enables the students to evaluate critically, design, and develop an effective data science system using contemporary tools and techniques.
  • Develop an understanding of the stakeholders’ perspective - consolidation of data sources, data preparation and aligning data to business objectives.
  • Develop an understanding of the cyber security risks that exist in the data management process.
  • Critically appraise the emerging trends, professional and ethical requirements for dealing with data science projects, and within which a Data Science professional must operate.

Data Analytics Report

Objective: Create a Data Analytics Report for Transport For Wales

Learning Outcomes

  • Critically analyse architecture, design, development methodology, querying and the lifecycle of managing large-scale datasets.
  • Distinguish between and critically reflect on the solutions of various data analytics approaches which support business decision making process.

Assignment Details: Transport for Wales has conducted a detailed survey to understand its citizen’s view on the public transport system. As a data scientist, the department has awarded you a data analysis contract. The information you supply will facilitate the executives taking necessary measures to improve the transportation systems in the country. Your first task is to write an initial "Data Analytics Report" which will cover your plans for data processing and evaluation.

Data Analytics Implementation

Objective: Undertake the analysis according to the suggested design in your Data Analytics Report.

Learning Outcomes

  • Distinguish between and critically reflect on the solutions of various data analytics approaches which support business decision making process.
  • Apply and evaluate critically the various methods, tools, technologies and success factors applied to a data science project in order to develop an effective plan and delivery of solutions to a business problem.

This assignment uses charts and graphs created with Python along with a written "Implementation document" that details the data analysis methods that were undertaken.
