About

My name is Danielle Hall, a data enthusiast originally from Spokane Valley, Washington. At the age of 14, I embarked on a transformative journey, relocating to the beautiful city of Hamar, Norway. This cross-cultural experience broadened my horizons and instilled in me a deep appreciation for diversity and new perspectives.

My academic journey led me to the United Kingdom, where I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Psychology. This field fascinated me with its exploration of human behavior and cognition, laying a strong foundation for my analytical thinking and research skills. After graduation, I embarked on a fulfilling career journey as a healthcare worker in a psychiatric ward. This role provided me with invaluable firsthand experience in understanding human dynamics and the complexities of mental health care. It was during the night shifts at the psychiatric ward that I discovered my passion for data science.

Driven by a thirst for knowledge and a desire to make a meaningful impact, I began delving into data science courses during my free time. I was captivated by the power of data to uncover insights, drive informed decisions, and transform industries. This newfound passion sparked a determination to transition into the field of data science. Currently, I am pursuing a part-time Master's degree in Data Science, fueling my curiosity and honing my skills in statistics, programming, and machine learning. This journey represents the intersection of my academic background in psychology, my professional experience in healthcare, and my fervent interest in leveraging data to drive positive change.

In my spare time, I enjoy immersing myself in nature, spending time with the my adorable cat, and indulging in creative pursuits like painting. I believe in the importance of maintaining a healthy work-life balance to foster personal growth and well-being. As I continue on this exhilarating journey in data science, I am eager to contribute my unique perspectives, interdisciplinary insights, and unwavering dedication to creating innovative solutions that empower individuals and communities alike.

Let's connect and embark on this exciting journey together!
